  No server, no install, no dependencies needed.

HOW TO CUSTOMIZE
────────────────
  1. TEXTS
     Open index.html in any text editor (Notepad,
     VS Code, Sublime...) and replace:
       - "Nexora" → your brand name
       - All paragraph texts → your content
       - Prices → your prices
       - Stats (12K+, 98%...) → your real numbers

  2. COLORS
     Open style.css and edit the variables at the top:
       --yellow:   #F5C542   ← your accent color
       --bg-1:     #141414   ← main background
       --text-1:   #F5F4F1   ← main text color
     Change these 3 lines → the whole site updates.

  3. FONTS
     The template uses "Inter" (modern sans-serif)
     and "Fraunces" (italic serif for emphasis).
     To change fonts, edit the Google Fonts link
     in index.html (line 8) and update --font in
     style.css (line ~50).

  4. LINKS
     Search for href="#" in index.html and replace
     with your real page URLs.

  5. SECTIONS TO REMOVE
     Each section is clearly labeled with comments
     like <!-- ═══ HERO ═══ --> in index.html.
     You can delete any full section block safely.
